About

Paveekit Mahanakorn

The first company of Paveekij Group was established since 1977 under the name of Paveekij Pte.,Ltd as Nissan UD dealer in Petchaburi province in Thailand. In the year 1988, Paveekit Mahanakorn Co., Ltd. was set up to be the dealer of Nissan UD Trucks in Bangkok and metropolis, located at 71 Moo 11, Bangna-Trad Road KM. 20, Bangchalong, Bangplee District, Samutprakarn Province. , with the vision that this location would be a prime location for future business hub as it is closed to Suwannabhum Airport, and Laemchabang Sea Port. Presently, Paveekit Mahanakorn Co., Ltd. is a parent company where support hire purchase and have subsidiary corporations for commercial vehicles sales in the group.

Asia Truck

Asia Truck Co., Ltd. was established in 2006 being the authorized dealer of Mercedez Benz trucks and buses situated at 335/9 Moo9 Bangna-Trad Road KM. 19, Bangchalong, Bangplee , Samutprakarn Province.

Paveekit Truck Sales

Paveekit Truck Sales Co., Ltd. was established in 2008 being the authorized dealer of Fuso trucks located at 71 Moo 11, Bangna-Trad Road KM. 20, Bangchalong, Bangplee District, Samutprakarn Province. Our objectives are to sell quality products, increase market shares of Fuso trucks and Mercedez Benz trucks and buses in transporter logistics segments as well as construction segment, provide the best after sales service with fully equipped service center for each brand.
